About MTRE

MTRE is a national level scholarship exam conducted online by IES Master. It offers a window of opportunity to students to be at par, and be one among the best engineering minds.

Scope

MTRE is not just another scholarship exam to help reduce financial burden, it offers an insight into what goes into making the toppers of GATE and Engineering Services Exam. The online platform used in MTRE matches the orientation and level of top engineering competitive exams.

Eligibility

MTRE is for students who have obtained their graduate or Master degree (B. Tech/M.Tech) or the students are in the third/final year of their engineering degree course in Civil Engineering stream.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online
  • Time Duration: 1Hr.
  • Total Marks: 100.
  • Total Question: 50 (2 Marks for each Question).
  • Negative Marking: 0.50 marks for every wrong answer.
  • Test can be written from anywhere using system (Desktop, Laptop) and web camera. Without webcam, test can not be taken.
  • Each Questions will be provided 4 alternative options.

Instructions

  •   The MTRE exam will start on 04 December, 2022 at 6:00 PM sharp. Students will not be allowed to take the MTRE Exam after 6:15 PM.
  •   Make sure that you have a computer system (desktop/laptop) with updated windows version, google chrome and working webcam.   Without webcam, you can not take the test
  •   Before taking the MTRE, make sure that your Internet connectivity is not interrupted during the exam.
  •   While writing MTRE, students are required to keep their system’s webcam turned on, and keep it running during the entire duration of   exam to ensure that only bonafide person is writing the exam.
  •   The candidate has the flexibility to jump over to any question while writing MTRE.
  •   Each question of the MTRE exam is allotted specific time duration to attempt. The allotted time for each question can be viewed on the   top right corner of the screen while writing MTRE.
  •   If a candidate fails to answer any particular question within the stipulated time, he will be automatically redirected to the next question   having balance time.
  •   If a candidate fails to submit his answer to any particular question by clicking on the ‘Save and Next’ button within the allotted time,   his response will not be saved.
  •   Once a candidate has attempted all the questions of MTRE, he has to click on the ‘Submit’ button to finish the test.
  •   If a candidate fails to complete the MTRE exam in allotted time, then his test will get submitted automatically.
  •   During the MTRE Test, all the candidates will be provided with a virtual scientific calculator on computer screen which has to be used   to do calculation while answering questions. No physical calculator will be allowed. The same applies to mobile phones and wrist   watches with calculation facilities.
Note: All the above-mentioned instructions are to be adhered in good faith, failing which your candidature will stand null and void.

FAQ's

  • Is there any fee for MTRE registration?

    Yes, candidates need to pay online a nominal fee of Rs. 50/- to register themselves for MTRE.

  • Is there any cash prizes for winners?

    No, there would not be any cash prize for winners. The winners would only get a discount of upto 100% of the tuition fee on the basis of the result of the scholarship test from a nationwide pool of applicants.

  • Will there be multiple time slots for MTRE? IES?

    No, there would not be multiple time slots to write MTRE.

  • Is MTRE an offline or online test? Are there any particular exam centres?

    No, MTRE is an online test. All you require is a desktop/laptop with updated windows, google chrome and a Web Camera and Internet connection.

  • I am an existing student of IES Master, and have paid my complete course fee. Will I get the discount amount in refund if I get Scholarship?

    Current year students and ex-students of IES Master Classroom Program would only be eligible for one discount whichever is maximum. The discounts will be applicable on Classroom Batches and Live Online Batches start during December,2022 to March, 2023 only.

  • I have already qualified for a discount through MTRE last year, but due to some reason I could not enrol myself for classroom or Live Online program. Can I write the MTRE this year to claim a different discount slab?

    Yes, students who have already qualified MTRE last year but somehow could not enrol for classroom program or Live Online Course are eligible to write MTRE this year to claim a different discount slab.

  • Is there any time limit to answer the MTRE question paper?

    Yes,There is a time limit of 1 hour to answer the complete MTRE question paper. Also, there would be a time limitation to answer each question.

  • Is there any individual minimum qualifying score for each section of MTRE question paper?

    No, there is no minimum qualifying score for availing MTRE discount.

  • When will be the result of MTRE announced?

    The result of MTRE would be announced within 5 working days from the date of exam. In case there is any change in the date of result announcement, candidates would be notified through SMS/e-mail/IES Master website/app.

  • Can I write MTRE exam on smartphone? syllabus?

    No, you can not write MTRE on your smartphone. It can be taken on Desktop/Laptop with updated windows, Google Chrome and active webcam only

  • When is MTRE scheduled to be conducted?

    MTRE is scheduled to be conducted on 04 December, 2022.

  • What is the exam pattern of MTRE?

    MTRE is an online mode test that can be written from anywhere using a system (Desktop or Laptop) with updated windows, Google Chrome Browser, Web camera and Internet connectivity. The questions will be of objective type. Each Question will have 4 alternative options.The total time duration for writing MTRE will be 1 hour. In total there will be 50 questions of 2 marks each. There would be negative marking of 0.50 marks for every wrong answer.

  • How many marks do I need to score to avail the discount/scholarship?

    There is no minimum cut-off. Merit list would be released post MTRE. Basing on the marks score, the discount/scholarship will be offered.

  • What if I face any technical glitch or Internet failure while writing MTRE?

    You have to make sure that Internet is working at the start and while submitting the test paper. Also, the Web camera is mandatory while writing MTRE. Failing which you would not be able to avail MTRE benefits. Also, you can write to mtre@iesmaster.org or call at 80100 09955.

  • What are the eligibility criteria for appearing in MTRE?

    MTRE is for students who have obtained their graduate degree (B.Tech) in Civil Engineering streams. The scholarship program is also open to those students who are in the third/final year of their engineering degree course in civil engineering.

  • What are the documents that will be required for MTRE registration?

    Registration for MTRE can only be completed online and documents required will be scanned images of passport size photograph, signature and ID proof.

  • Will I need a Login User Name and Password to write MTRE?

    Yes, you would be requiring a User Name and Password to write MTRE. The User Name and Password will be generated at the time of registering for MTRE, and the same would be sent to you through SMS/E-mail.

  • What if I forget my password for MTRE Website Login?

    In case you have forgotten your password, you can click on ‘Forgot Password’, and then follow the process to reset your password. You would also receive the new password set by you on your registered mobile no./e-mail ID.

  • Can I edit my Profile information after registering for MTRE?

    You can change your photograph and signature only. Other than these two things, you cannot change any other information.

  • What if my test ends abruptly?

    The only thing you have to make sure is that you do not quit the MTRE test panel by pressing Back button, Refresh button, close the test window or opening a new tab. If your test freezes or ends due to any other reason will not be considered as submission of the test.

  • Can I use calculator?

    Yes, you can use a calculator while writing MTRE.

  • Who should I contact in case my queries are not resolved here?

    To get your queries or issues related to MTRE answered, you can write to mtre@iesmaster.org or call at 80100 09955.
